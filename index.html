Welcome to Experation

This is a guide for you to get the optimal use of this app.

Read on and see how

Your Fridge

Here you can see an inventory of the food you currently have in your fridge.

This symbol: gives you the opportunity to add by search directly to your fridge

The symbol that looks like this placed in the right corner will get you directly to your shopping list

At each food item you will see two symbols:

With this one you can move the selected item to your shopping list.

This symbol will just remove it from the fridge.

Shopping list

On this site see the food items you placed here.

Check of when you have bought an item and it will get placed in the fridge

Click on this symbol if you regret the item and don't actually want it

Again this symbol will add items directly by search this time to your shopping list instead

With this symbol you'll get you to the fridge site, you can find it in the right corner.

Add food

On this site you get a few options to find food from here you can add both to your fridge and shopping list.

With this symbol you can directly type in the item you want, this you'll find in the right corner of this site.

Another option is to scan your food and add that directly to the fridge
